About Our Company

    Welcome to Synbiome, where we are pioneering the future of healthcare with innovative technologies that merge biotechnology, nanotechnology, and AI. At the heart of our mission is the Synbiome BioCapsule—a groundbreaking device equipped with intelligent nanorobots for targeted drug delivery, real-time physiological monitoring, and secure health management. Our vision is to transform disease treatment and health optimization, empowering individuals with personalized, precision medicine in a way that is both effective and secure. Join us in shaping the future of healthcare.

Synbiome BioCapsule-3: A New Era of Precision Medicine

A nanorobot-driven smart capsule providing precise medical solutions, tracking vital physiological indicators such as blood glucose, inflammation, and neurotransmitters in real-time.

Core Functions:

  • Nanorobot-enabled capsule: Equipped with thousands of intelligent nanorobots, capable of precise drug delivery, micro-level tissue repair, and real-time monitoring of cellular status, exceeding traditional bacteria-based approaches.
  • Real-time monitoring of host physiological indicators: Continuously tracks crucial physiological metrics such as blood glucose, inflammatory factors, and neurotransmitters, providing real-time health insights for early detection of potential issues.
  • Advanced anti-hacking function: Integrated with multi-layer encryption, real-time anomaly detection, and self-recovery mechanisms to ensure the security and integrity of the system against external threats.
